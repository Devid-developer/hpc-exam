Serial optimization

Making the Hot Loop Compiler-Friendly

Before: repeated index arithmetic
new[IDX(i,j)] = cc * old[IDX(i,j)]
  + cn * (old[IDX(i-1,j)]
        + old[IDX(i+1,j)]
        + old[IDX(i,j-1)]
        + old[IDX(i,j+1)]);
After: regular row access
result[i] = cc * center[i]
  + cn * (north[i] + south[i]
        + center[i-1] + center[i+1]);

Less work

Hoisted coefficients and row offsets.

Clear semantics

restrict, size_t, double buffering.

SIMD-friendly

64-byte alignment and contiguous row-major access.
